Guests

Think about what happens when you check into a hotel today. They ask your name, your preferences, whether you have stayed before. Even if the answer is yes, twelve times.

HAILO gives every guest a single profile that lives across every hotel in the world. Your history, your preferences, the people who made your stay special. All travel with you permanently. After a few stays, you browse hotels on HAILO, see verified scores from real guests, and book direct. The relationship with every hotel you visit never resets again.

Hotels

A general manager walks in and knows exactly what is happening in every corner of their hotel. Because HAILO already showed them.

Live feeds by department. Real guest sentiment across every interaction. Requests routed to the right person in seconds. Which teams are thriving, which need attention. The information arrives before anyone needs to ask for it. Decisions are made on what is actually happening, not on what was summarised over coffee.

Careers

There are people working in hotels right now who are extraordinary at what they do. Guests remember them by name. The industry has given them no way to prove it.

HAILO builds a verified career portfolio from real guest interactions. Every positive moment, every recognition, every name request becomes part of a permanent record that belongs to the person who earned it. When they move to a new hotel, loyal guests are notified. A hospitality career is finally something you can see, carry with you, and build on.

Corporate Travel

A travel manager books rooms for twenty colleagues and watches their entire trip unfold. What took days of emails takes three messages.

Smart billing applied automatically. Dietary requirements pushed to every restaurant the moment the booking is confirmed. If something goes wrong during the stay, the alert arrives immediately. The post-stay report downloads in one message. The travel manager sees everything while it is happening, not in an expense report weeks later.

Airlines

An airline sends its most valued passengers to a partner hotel. For the first time, they know exactly what happens next.

Quality dashboards across every partner property in real time. Alerts when a premium passenger is underserved. Route-level analysis showing which flights produce the most fatigued guests who need the most attention on arrival. Partnership decisions driven by verified data instead of complaint letters that arrive months too late.

Card Programmes

Amex, Visa, and Mastercard promise benefits with every stay. HAILO confirms that every single one was delivered.

Room upgrades, early check-in, late checkout, breakfast, experience credits. Every benefit tracked, timestamped at the moment of delivery, and reported automatically. If something was promised and not provided, the programme knows within hours, not after a dispute is filed months later. Cardholder trust protected in real time.

Concierge & Travel

A concierge company looks after hundreds of elite clients across the world. With HAILO, the care scales without losing the personal touch.

Every client's preferences arrive at every hotel before they do. Live monitoring across every active stay. If something goes wrong, the alert reaches the right person in seconds. One lifestyle manager can oversee what used to require an entire team of coordinators making phone calls around the clock.

Events

A wedding planner coordinates with five hotel departments for one evening. HAILO connects them all in a single conversation.

Conferences, galas, corporate retreats, celebrations. Every event involves the kitchen, the banquet team, AV, housekeeping, and guest relations working at the same time. HAILO gives the event planner a live view across every department involved. Requests flow to the right people instantly. Changes reach everyone in seconds. The coordination that used to take dozens of calls happens in one connected thread.

Tour Operators

Forty travellers arrive at a hotel on the same afternoon. Each with different needs. The hotel sees every single one of them clearly.

Group travel has always meant generic service. Everyone gets the same room, the same welcome, the same buffet. HAILO gives the tour operator a way to share individual preferences for every guest in the group before they arrive. Dietary needs, accessibility requirements, room preferences, celebration dates. The hotel receives it all automatically and serves forty individuals, not one group number.

Health & Wellness

A guest travels across the world for medical treatment. Their hotel stay is part of their recovery. HAILO makes sure the hotel understands that.

Medical tourism is growing rapidly across the UAE and MENA region. Guests recovering from procedures need specific room configurations, carefully managed dietary requirements, accessibility accommodations, and a level of care that goes beyond standard hospitality. HAILO allows the medical coordinator to communicate directly with the hotel so that every detail of the guest's wellbeing is handled with precision from the moment they arrive.
